The Ikshvaku Manifesto

Research • Discipline • Compounding

Why We Exist

Ikshvaku Research exists to build a permanent public record of capital allocation. We believe investment decisions should be documented, studied, measured and improved.

The objective is not simply to make money. The objective is to develop sound judgment and compound knowledge, trust and capital over decades.

We Believe In Research

Research reduces uncertainty. Understanding businesses, industries, incentives and risks creates an advantage that cannot be obtained through speculation.

Every investment begins with research.

We Believe In Process

Good outcomes can come from bad decisions. Bad outcomes can come from good decisions.

Therefore we focus on process rather than short-term results. A repeatable process compounds over time.

We Believe In Transparency

Trust is earned through transparency. Research, decisions, performance and lessons should be documented honestly.

History should not be rewritten because outcomes were disappointing.

We Believe In Accountability

Every investment decision should be recorded. Every mistake should be reviewed. Every lesson should be preserved.

Accountability creates credibility.

We Believe In Long-Term Thinking

Markets reward patience. Businesses create value over years. Capital compounds over decades.

We seek to think in years rather than days.

Our Commitment

We commit to research.

We commit to discipline.

We commit to transparency.

We commit to continuous learning.

We commit to building an institution that endures.

Final Principle

Research compounds. Knowledge compounds. Trust compounds. Capital compounds.

The purpose of Ikshvaku Research is to compound all four.
