About Ikshvaku Research

Ikshvaku Research is an independent investment research platform dedicated to the study of businesses, capital allocation, and long-term wealth creation.

The purpose of this website is to document research, investment ideas, portfolio development, and lessons learned throughout the journey of becoming a better investor.

Our Mission

To build a disciplined investment process based on research, rational decision-making, and continuous learning while maintaining a transparent public record of progress.

What We Do

  • Analyze listed companies and business models
  • Study financial statements and annual reports
  • Track portfolio decisions and performance
  • Document investment research and market observations
  • Share lessons learned from investing and business analysis

Investment Philosophy

Our approach is based on five core principles:

  • Research First – Every investment decision begins with understanding the business.
  • Long-Term Thinking – Wealth is created through patience and compounding.
  • Risk Management – Preserving capital is as important as growing it.
  • Continuous Learning – Markets evolve and investors must evolve with them.
  • Discipline – Consistent execution matters more than short-term predictions.

Vision

The long-term vision of Ikshvaku Research is to develop a credible public track record, deepen investment expertise, and create a foundation for future growth in investment research and asset management.
