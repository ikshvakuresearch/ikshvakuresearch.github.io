Constitution

The Operating Principles of Ikshvaku Research

Article I — Purpose

Ikshvaku Research exists to compound capital through disciplined research, rational capital allocation, and long-term decision making while maintaining the highest standards of accountability and transparency.

The institution seeks to build a permanent public record of investment research, decisions, outcomes, and lessons.

Article II — Capital Allocation

  • Capital preservation comes before capital growth.
  • Position sizes should reflect conviction and risk.
  • Cash is considered a valid investment position.
  • Every investment must have documented reasoning.
  • Long-term compounding is prioritized over short-term speculation.

Article III — Risk Management

  • Permanent loss of capital should be avoided whenever possible.
  • Every investment must include a risk assessment.
  • Portfolio concentration should be monitored.
  • Major mistakes must be documented and reviewed.
  • Risk management is an ongoing process.

Article IV — Research Standards

Every published research report should include:

  • Business Overview
  • Industry Analysis
  • Competitive Advantage Assessment
  • Management Evaluation
  • Financial Analysis
  • Valuation Analysis
  • Risk Assessment
  • Investment Thesis
  • Conclusion

Article V — Transparency

  • Research reports shall remain publicly accessible.
  • Performance records shall be maintained consistently.
  • Closed investment positions shall be documented.
  • Methodology changes shall be disclosed.
  • History shall not be rewritten to improve appearances.

Article VI — Long-Term Objectives

2030

Build a respected public research and performance archive.

2035

Develop institutional-grade research and investment processes.

2040

Establish professional governance and operational systems.

2046

Build Ikshvaku Research into a respected capital allocation institution with decades of documented decisions and outcomes.

Article VII — Core Values

  • Discipline
  • Patience
  • Integrity
  • Accountability
  • Transparency
  • Continuous Learning
  • Long-Term Thinking

Article VIII — Amendments

This Constitution may evolve as Ikshvaku Research grows.

Any material amendment should be documented publicly and preserved within the institution's historical record.
