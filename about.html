Who We Are

Ikshvaku Research is an independent investment research platform focused on understanding businesses, studying capital allocation decisions and documenting the journey of becoming a better long-term investor.

The platform serves as a transparent public record of research, portfolio development, market observations and lessons learned through continuous study and practical investing experience.

Why Ikshvaku Research Exists

Financial markets reward discipline, patience and rational decision-making. Ikshvaku Research was created to build a structured investment framework while documenting that journey publicly.

The goal is not to predict short-term market movements but to develop a repeatable process based on business quality, valuation, risk management and long-term thinking.

Our Mission

To develop a disciplined investment process based on research, independent thinking and continuous learning while maintaining transparency and accountability through a publicly documented track record.

What We Do

  • Analyze public companies and business models
  • Study annual reports and financial statements
  • Track portfolio decisions and performance
  • Publish investment research reports
  • Document market observations and lessons learned
  • Build a transparent public investment record

Investment Philosophy

  • Research First — Every investment begins with understanding the business.
  • Long-Term Thinking — Wealth is created through patience and compounding.
  • Risk Management — Protecting capital is as important as growing it.
  • Continuous Learning — Markets evolve and investors must evolve with them.
  • Discipline — Consistent execution matters more than prediction.

Core Principles

  • Independent Thinking
  • Long-Term Capital Allocation
  • Business Quality Focus
  • Risk-Aware Decision Making
  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Continuous Improvement

Our Approach

Ikshvaku Research follows a business-owner mindset when evaluating investments. The focus is on understanding business quality, management capability, competitive advantages and long-term growth potential.

Investment decisions are guided by research, valuation discipline and risk management rather than market speculation or short-term trends.

The objective is to compound capital responsibly while continuously improving the investment process.

Long-Term Vision

The long-term vision of Ikshvaku Research is to build a respected investment research platform with a transparent public track record.

Over time, the platform aims to expand from publishing research and portfolio updates into a broader investment-focused organization dedicated to capital allocation, research excellence and investor education.

Every report, investment decision and portfolio update contributes to building that foundation.
