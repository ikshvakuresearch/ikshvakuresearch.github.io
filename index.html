Independent Investment Research

Ikshvaku Research is an independent investment research organization focused on business analysis, capital allocation, transparency and long-term wealth creation.

Our Mission

To build a transparent, accountable and disciplined investment institution through rigorous research, rational capital allocation and long-term compounding.

What We Do

  • Publish independent research reports.
  • Manage and track personal investment capital.
  • Document investment decisions publicly.
  • Maintain transparent performance records.
  • Build a long-term institutional track record.

Our Investment Philosophy

We believe wealth is created through ownership of high-quality businesses, disciplined capital allocation, patience and continuous learning.

Every research report, portfolio decision and performance update is documented to create accountability and credibility over time.
