Who We Are

Ikshvaku Research is an independent investment research platform dedicated to studying businesses, capital allocation and long-term wealth creation.

The website serves as a public record of research, investment ideas, portfolio development, market observations and lessons learned throughout the investing journey.

Why Ikshvaku Research Exists

Financial markets reward discipline, patience and continuous learning. Ikshvaku Research was created to document the process of studying businesses, making investment decisions and building long-term wealth through informed capital allocation.

The objective is not to predict short-term market movements but to develop a repeatable investment process based on research, rational thinking and continuous improvement.

Our Mission

To develop a disciplined investment process based on research, rational decision-making and continuous learning while maintaining transparency and accountability through a publicly documented journey.

What We Do

  • Analyze listed companies and business models
  • Study annual reports and financial statements
  • Track portfolio decisions and performance
  • Publish investment research reports
  • Document market observations and lessons learned
  • Build a transparent public investment record

Investment Philosophy

  • Research First – Every investment begins with understanding the business.
  • Long-Term Thinking – Wealth is created through patience and compounding.
  • Risk Management – Protecting capital is as important as growing it.
  • Continuous Learning – Markets evolve and investors must evolve with them.
  • Discipline – Consistent execution matters more than prediction.

Founder Letter

Ikshvaku Research began with a simple idea: learn publicly, think independently and improve continuously as an investor.

Every research report, journal entry and portfolio update represents another step toward building a disciplined investment framework and a long-term track record.

The journey is ongoing, and this platform exists to document progress with honesty, transparency and intellectual curiosity.

Long-Term Vision

Over time, Ikshvaku Research aims to build a publicly documented track record of investment research, portfolio management and capital allocation decisions.

The long-term ambition is to evolve from an independent research platform into a respected investment-focused organization built on transparency, discipline and continuous learning.
