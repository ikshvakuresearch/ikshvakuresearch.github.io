About Ikshvaku Research

Independent Investment Research

Who We Are

Ikshvaku Research is an independent investment research platform dedicated to studying businesses, capital allocation and long-term wealth creation.

The website serves as a public record of research, investment ideas, portfolio development and lessons learned throughout the investing journey.

Our Mission

To develop a disciplined investment process based on research, rational decision-making and continuous learning while maintaining transparency and accountability.

What We Do

  • Analyze public companies and business models
  • Study annual reports and financial statements
  • Track investment decisions and portfolio performance
  • Publish research reports and market observations
  • Document lessons learned from investing

Investment Philosophy

  • Research First – Understand the business before investing.
  • Long-Term Thinking – Wealth is built through patience and compounding.
  • Risk Management – Protecting capital is essential.
  • Continuous Learning – Markets evolve and investors must evolve with them.
  • Discipline – Consistency matters more than prediction.

Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal of Ikshvaku Research is to build a credible public track record, deepen investment expertise and create a foundation for future growth in investment research and asset management.

Disclaimer

All information published on this website is provided for educational and research purposes only and should not be considered investment advice.
