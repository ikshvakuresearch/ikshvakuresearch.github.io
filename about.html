Who We Are

Ikshvaku Research is an independent investment research platform dedicated to the study of businesses, capital allocation, portfolio management and long-term wealth creation.

The objective of the platform is to develop a disciplined investment process through continuous learning, independent thinking and evidence-based decision making.

Rather than focusing on market predictions or short-term price movements, Ikshvaku Research emphasizes business quality, financial strength, valuation discipline and long-term ownership thinking.

The platform also serves as a transparent public record of research, portfolio decisions, investment observations and lessons learned throughout the journey of becoming a better investor.

Why Ikshvaku Research Exists

Financial markets reward patience, discipline and rational decision-making. However, developing these qualities requires years of study, experience and continuous improvement.

Ikshvaku Research was created to document that journey publicly.

The goal is not to provide stock tips or predict market movements. The goal is to build a repeatable investment framework, improve decision quality and create a long-term record of research and capital allocation decisions.

By documenting both successes and mistakes, the platform aims to encourage accountability, intellectual honesty and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

To build a disciplined investment process based on research, independent thinking and continuous learning while maintaining transparency and accountability through a publicly documented track record.

Every report, portfolio update and journal entry is intended to contribute to a deeper understanding of investing, business analysis and capital allocation.

Our Approach

Ikshvaku Research follows a business-owner mindset when evaluating investments.

The focus is on understanding business quality, management capability, competitive advantages, capital allocation decisions and long-term growth potential.

Investment decisions are guided by research, valuation discipline and risk management rather than speculation or short-term market trends.

The objective is to compound capital responsibly while continuously improving the investment process.

Long-Term Vision

The long-term vision of Ikshvaku Research is to build a respected investment research platform with a transparent and publicly documented track record.

Over time, the platform aims to evolve from a personal research archive into a recognized source of thoughtful business analysis, portfolio management insights and investor education.

Every research report, portfolio update and journal entry contributes to building that foundation.
