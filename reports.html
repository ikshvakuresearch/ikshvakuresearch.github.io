Research Methodology

Every report published by Ikshvaku Research follows a structured framework designed to improve decision quality and reduce emotional decision-making.


  • Business Understanding — Study products, services, industry structure and competitive positioning.
  • Financial Analysis — Review revenue growth, profitability, cash flow and balance sheet strength.
  • Management Evaluation — Assess leadership quality, capital allocation and shareholder orientation.
  • Risk Assessment — Identify operational, financial, regulatory and industry risks.
  • Valuation Analysis — Estimate intrinsic value and expected long-term return potential.
  • Investment Conclusion — Document investment thesis, risks and key assumptions.

Publication Process

Research reports are published only after completing the full research framework. The objective is quality over quantity.
